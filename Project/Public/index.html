Learn Hindi Words

Embark on a linguistic journey! Test your skills, improve your vocabulary, and have fun learning Hindi!

How to Play

  • Identify whether words are nouns or verbs
  • Press 'N' for nouns, 'V' for verbs
  • Use 'H' key for hints
  • You have 3 attempts per game
  • Score points to reach higher levels
